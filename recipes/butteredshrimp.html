Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ang sarap sarap ng garlic buttered shrimp. Madali lang siyang gawin.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Red pepper
  • White wine
  • Fresh Herbs

Steps

  1. Dry the Shrimp and Add the Seasonings.
  2. Melt the Butter.
  3. Cook the Shrimp. Flip and cook on the other side.
  4. Add the Wine and Garlic. Continue cooking.
  5. Stir in the Remaining Ingredients. Serve immediately with lemon wedges. ENJOY!
