Garlic Parmesan

Ang sarap sarap ng garlic parmesan. Madali lang siyang gawin.

Ingredients

  • salted butter or ghee, melted
  • fresh garlic, minced
  • garlic powder
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded and finely chopped
  • chopped parsley

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, heat the butter in the microwave until just melted. You can also use a small saucepan on the stovetop.
  2. Stir in the minced garlic, parmesan, parsley, and garlic powder.
  3. Reserve Â¼ cup of the mixture to make the wing dipping sauce.
  4. When the wings are done and ready to be tossed in the sauce, add the wing sauce to a large bowl and toss the baked wings until they are evenly coated.
  5. Plate the wings and garnish with chopped parsley. DIG IN.
